Section 5: Family Sharing Feature

Please read this section carefully before enabling Family Sharing.

5.1 What is Family Sharing?

PhotoVault offers an optional Family Sharing feature that allows you to give family members access to view and download photos in your galleries. This feature is designed to help families preserve and share precious memories together, making it easy for multiple generations to access and enjoy your family's photo collection.

5.2 Access Levels

When you enable Family Sharing, you can grant access at the following levels:

  • Account Owner: Full administrative control, billing management, ability to authorize family members, create/delete galleries
  • Family Admin: Can view and download all shared photos, add photos to shared galleries, invite additional family members (with Owner approval)
  • Family Viewer: Can view and download shared photos only

5.3 Platform Philosophy: Designed for Family Memories

PhotoVault is designed for family memories. We built Family Sharing to make it effortless for families to preserve and access their shared photo history across generations. When grandparents, parents, and children can all access the same family photos, those memories stay alive and connected.

This means you should think carefully about what you upload. Photos stored in galleries with Family Sharing enabled may be accessible to the family members you authorize. Do not upload sensitive, private, or confidential images that you would not want your authorized family members to access.

By enabling the Family Sharing feature, you consent to authorized family members having access to view and download photos in your shared galleries. If you require private, individual-only storage, we recommend keeping Family Sharing disabled or using a separate gallery that is not shared.

5.4 Your Responsibilities with Family Sharing

  • You are responsible for choosing which family members to authorize
  • You are responsible for choosing which galleries to share
  • You may revoke family member access at any time from your account settings
  • You understand that authorized family members can download shared photos
  • PhotoVault is not responsible for how authorized family members use downloaded photos

5.5 What Family Sharing is NOT For

Family Sharing is not appropriate for:

  • Private or confidential images you don't want family to see
  • Sensitive personal photos intended for individual viewing only
  • Business or commercial content
  • Content subject to confidentiality agreements

For these use cases, keep Family Sharing disabled or use galleries that are not shared with family members.
