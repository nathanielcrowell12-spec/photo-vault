## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [photovault.photo](https://photovault.photo)
2. Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the link to verify your account

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

After signing up, you'll be guided through onboarding:
- Add your business name
- Upload a profile photo (optional)
- Set your contact preferences

## Connecting Your Stripe Account

**Why is this required?** Stripe handles all payments securely. When clients pay for their galleries, your earnings are deposited directly into your bank account.

### How to Connect Stripe

1. Go to **Settings** in your dashboard
2. Click **"Connect Stripe Account"**
3. You'll be redirected to Stripe's secure signup
4. Enter your business and banking details
5. Once complete, you'll be redirected back to PhotoVault

### What You Need for Stripe

- Legal business name (or your full name if sole proprietor)
- Business address
- Bank account details (routing + account number)
- Social Security Number or EIN (for tax reporting)
- Phone number for verification

### Stripe Account Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Connected** | Ready to receive payments |
| **Pending** | Stripe is verifying your information |
| **Action Required** | Log into Stripe to provide additional info |

## Creating Your First Gallery

### Step 1: Click "Create Gallery"

From your dashboard, click the **"Create Gallery"** button.

### Step 2: Enter Gallery Details

- **Gallery Name**: Something descriptive (e.g., "Smith Wedding - June 2025")
- **Description** (optional): Notes about the shoot
- **Event Date** (optional): When the photos were taken

### Step 3: Set Pricing

PhotoVault offers two package options for your clients:

| Package | Client Pays | You Earn |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Year Package** | $100 upfront | $50 commission |
| **6-Month Package** | $50 upfront | $25 commission |

After the initial package, clients pay $8/month to maintain access. **You earn $4/month** from each active client.

### Step 4: Save and Continue

Click **"Create Gallery"** to save. Now you're ready to upload photos!

## Uploading Photos

### Option 1: Desktop App (Recommended for Large Uploads)

The PhotoVault Desktop App handles large uploads reliably, even with slow internet.

**Download:** [PhotoVault Desktop App](https://photovault.photo/download)

**Features:**
- Upload ZIP files up to 10GB
- Automatic resume if connection drops
- Background uploading
- Progress tracking

**How to Use:**
1. Download and install the app
2. Sign in with your PhotoVault account
3. Select a gallery
4. Drag and drop your ZIP file
5. Wait for upload and processing to complete

### Option 2: Web Upload (For Smaller Batches)

For smaller uploads (under 100 photos), you can upload directly in your browser:

1. Open the gallery you created
2. Click **"Upload Photos"**
3. Select files from your computer
4. Wait for upload to complete

### Supported Formats

- JPEG / JPG
- PNG
- HEIC (converted automatically)
- RAW files (coming soon)

### Photo Processing

After upload, PhotoVault automatically:
- Generates web-optimized versions
- Creates thumbnails for fast browsing
- Preserves original quality files for download

## Inviting Clients

Once your gallery has photos, invite your client to view and pay:

### Step 1: Get the Gallery Link

1. Open the gallery
2. Click **"Share"** or **"Invite Client"**
3. Copy the unique gallery link

### Step 2: Send to Your Client

Share the link via:
- Email
- Text message
- Your booking software
- Social media DM

### What Your Client Sees

When clients click the link, they can:
1. Preview a few teaser photos
2. Choose their package (Year or 6-Month)
3. Create an account and pay
4. Instantly access all photos

## Managing Your Subscription

### Platform Fee

PhotoVault charges **$22/month** for the platform. This covers:
- Unlimited galleries
- Unlimited photo storage
- Client management tools
- Commission payouts
- Customer support

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **"Update Payment Method"**
3. Enter new card details
4. Click **"Save"**

### Canceling Your Subscription

We're sad to see you go! To cancel:

1. Go to **Settings > Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Confirm cancellation

**Note:** Your galleries remain accessible to clients even if you cancel. You'll continue earning commissions from existing clients.

## Tips for Success

### 1. Deliver Quickly

Clients are excited right after their shoot. Deliver within 1-2 weeks while excitement is high.

### 2. Use Descriptive Gallery Names

Help clients find their galleries easily: "Johnson Family - Fall 2025" beats "Gallery_123".

### 3. Include a Personal Touch

Add a description thanking them for the shoot. It makes the delivery feel special.

### 4. Promote the Storage Benefit

Remind clients that PhotoVault stores their photos forever - no more lost USBs or failed hard drives.

### 5. Set Up Family Sharing

Encourage clients to invite family members. Grandparents love having access to family photos!
