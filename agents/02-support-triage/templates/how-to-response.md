## Common How-To Responses

### Upload Photos
```
1. Click "Upload" in the top right corner
2. Select your gallery or create a new one
3. Drag and drop your photos or click "Choose Files"
4. Wait for upload to complete (you'll see a green checkmark)
5. Your photos are now safely stored!
```

### Share a Gallery
```
1. Open the gallery you want to share
2. Click the "Share" button at the top
3. Choose "Create Share Link"
4. Copy the link and send it to your client
5. They can view and download photos without logging in!
```

### Download Photos
```
1. Open the gallery containing your photos
2. Select the photos you want (or click "Select All")
3. Click "Download" button
4. Choose quality (Original or Web size)
5. Your photos will download as a ZIP file
```

### Organize Galleries
```
1. Go to your Dashboard
2. Click "Organize" in the sidebar
3. Drag galleries to reorder them
4. Create folders by clicking "New Folder"
5. Drag galleries into folders to organize
```

### Change Account Settings
```
1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Settings"
3. Choose the section you want to update (Profile, Billing, Preferences)
4. Make your changes
5. Click "Save Changes"
```

### Invite Clients
```
1. Open the gallery you want to share
2. Click "Invite Client"
3. Enter their email address
4. Add a personal message (optional)
5. Click "Send Invitation"
```

## When to Use

- Question starts with "How do I...", "How can I...", "Can I..."
- Clear match to KB article exists
- Simple, straightforward answer
- No account-specific issues

## When NOT to Use

- Question is actually a bug report ("I tried to upload but it failed")
- Requires account investigation ("My photos disappeared")
- Multiple related questions in one ticket
- Customer already tried the steps and failed
